About

Eighteen years of listening first, treating second.

The story behind Cardia Heart Centre, and the physician who built it around a simple idea: patients deserve time.

Dr. Ananya Sharma, MD FACC

Trained in precision. Practiced in patience.

Dr. Sharma completed her cardiology fellowship at PGIMER Chandigarh after earning her MD from Christian Medical College (CMC), Ludhiana. She has spent the past eighteen years treating patients across the full spectrum of cardiovascular care — from first-time risk screenings to complex post-surgical recovery.

She founded Cardia Heart Centre in 2014 with a single goal: to build a practice where patients never feel like a number on a chart. Today, the practice serves over 6,000 patients across Ludhiana and the surrounding Punjab region.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Sharma teaches a continuing-education seminar on preventive cardiology and volunteers with a regional heart-health screening initiative for underserved communities in rural Punjab.
