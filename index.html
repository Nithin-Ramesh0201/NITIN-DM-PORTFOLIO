Heart care that listens before it treats.

Patient-First Approach

Unhurried appointments with plain-language explanations in Hindi or Punjabi — a plan you actually understand before you leave the room.

Precision Diagnostics

In-house echocardiography, stress testing, Holter monitoring, and CT angiography — results reviewed personally, same-day reports available.

Continuity of Care

Areas of Care

Care across every stage of heart health.

01

Preventive Cardiology

Risk assessment, cholesterol and blood sugar management, and lifestyle planning tailored to Punjabi dietary habits and family history.

02

Diagnostic Testing

ECG, echocardiogram, TMT stress test, 24-hr Holter monitoring, and lipid profiling — same-week scheduling, reports in Hindi if needed.

03

Interventional Care

Angiography, angioplasty, and stenting with post-procedure cardiac rehab, coordinated with DMC & Hospital and Dayanand Medical College, Ludhiana.
